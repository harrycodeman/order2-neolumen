Spark Theme

Happy New Year 2011 guys! We are starting the 2011 theme collection with our new “Spark” theme. Inspired by all the sparks and glows of new years fireworks its glamourous look fits perfect for fashion or nightlife websites.

Spark is a dark themed template which comes with a light and clean designed layout. The theme backgrounds are animated with some spectacular glitter and glow effects. We used different image masks and background tiles which are smoothly animated with JavaScript for the theme variations. These shiny background effects are a real eye-catcher! If you like it simple with no background animations you can easily turn them off in the theme configuration.
